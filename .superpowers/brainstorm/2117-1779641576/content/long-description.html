Chrome Web Store — full description

If you're using ChatGPT or Claude to tailor your resume and cover letters, you already know the drill: find a job posting, copy the description, paste it into AI, switch tabs, repeat. JobSearch Aide cuts out all of that.

One click from any job listing saves everything you need — no forms to fill, no new accounts, no subscription.

What it does:

  • Logs the job to your Google Sheet — company, role, link, and status, in the spreadsheet you already own
  • Saves an AI-ready copy of the job description — downloaded as a clean text file (.md) you can paste straight into any AI tool, no reformatting needed
  • Logs outreach contacts from LinkedIn — track who you've messaged without leaving the page

How it works:

Connect your Google Sheet once, then forget the setup. Every time you find a role worth saving, click the extension, confirm the details, and hit Save. The job appears in your spreadsheet and the description lands in your downloads folder, ready for AI.

Built for:

Job seekers doing an active, organized search who use AI to customize their applications — and want their data in their own spreadsheet, not locked in someone else's app.

Also included (beta):

Autofill for common application form fields from a saved profile. Works on most Greenhouse-hosted job applications. Support for other platforms is in progress.
