What should the extension be called?

Targeting non-tech job seekers who use AI to customize applications. Pick any that feel right — or none.

A

JobSaver

Plain, literal, instantly understood. "Save job listings to your spreadsheet." No jargon. Lowest friction for non-tech users.

B

Shortlist

Familiar job-search vocabulary. Implies curation and organization. Slightly more aspirational — you're not just tracking, you're being strategic.

C

JobPrompt

Leans into the AI angle — you're creating prompts from job listings. Might read as too techy for the target user, but it's distinctive.

D

ApplyStash

Captures the "grab and store" behavior. "Stash" implies a personal, organized collection. A bit playful without being cutesy.

Or type your own idea in the terminal — these are just starting points.
