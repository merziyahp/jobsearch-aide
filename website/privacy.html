Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

What this extension does

JobSearch Aide is a Chrome extension that helps you organize your job search. It saves job listing details to a Google Sheet you configure, downloads a copy of the job description to your local downloads folder, and lets you log outreach contacts to a second sheet.

What data we access

  • Google Sheets: We access only the specific Google Sheet(s) you connect during setup. We read and write job listing and outreach data to those sheets. We do not access any other files in your Google account.
  • Active browser tab: When you click the extension, we read the content of the current page to extract job listing information. We do not store or transmit your browsing history.
  • Downloads: We save job description files to your local downloads folder. These files never leave your device.
  • Local storage: We store your extension settings (Sheet URL, tab name, autofill profile) locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device.

What we do not do

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.
  • We do not track your usage or run any analytics.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • All data written by this extension goes to your own Google Sheet or your local machine — nowhere else.

Equal opportunity information

If you use the autofill feature and enter equal opportunity information (gender, race, disability status, veteran status), that data is stored only in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local). It is never synced across devices and is never transmitted anywhere.

Third-party services

This extension uses the Google Sheets API to read and write data to your Google Sheet. Your use of Google Sheets is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
